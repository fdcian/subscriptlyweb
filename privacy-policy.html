Subscriptly Privacy Policy

Subscriptly is designed to keep your subscription data on your device. This page explains what we store, how permissions are used, and the controls you have.

What we collect
Your controls

What data Subscriptly handles

We keep your information minimal and on-device. Subscriptly does not run accounts or cloud sync.

Subscription details

Names, prices, categories, renewal dates, and notes you add are stored locally on your device.

Local storage only

Data stays on your iPhone or iPad. We do not operate servers, and nothing is uploaded to us.

Notifications

Reminder notifications are scheduled on-device. You can turn them on or off in Subscriptly or system settings.

Calendar access

If you choose to sync, Subscriptly creates renewal events in Apple Calendar. We do not read other calendar data.

Privacy basics and how we handle your data

Subscriptly is built to keep subscription tracking simple and private. There are no accounts, and we do not collect analytics or personal identifiers.

Permissions

Notifications and Calendar access are optional and only used to deliver reminders you request.

Third parties

We do not sell or share your subscription data. The app does not include third-party trackers.

Backups

If you enable iCloud device backups, your data is backed up with your device according to Apple’s settings.

Children

Subscriptly is built for general audiences and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

If we ever change how data is handled, we will update this page and the in-app notice so you can review before continuing.
